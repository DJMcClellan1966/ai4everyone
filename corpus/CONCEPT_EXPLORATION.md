# Concept Exploration with AI Learning Companion

## What is a "Concept"?

In the context of the AI Learning Companion, a **concept** is a fundamental ML/AI idea that you can learn about.

   - Learn: `learn supervised_learning`

3. **classification**
   - What: Predicting categories
   - Learn: `learn classification`

4. **regression**
   - What: Predicting continuous values
   - Learn: `learn regression`

1. **neural_networks**
   - What: Brain-inspired models
   - Learn: `learn neural_networks`

2. **deep_learning**
   - What: Multi-layer neural networks
   - Learn: `learn deep_learning`

   - What: Creating good inputs
   - Learn: `learn feature_engineering`

### **Advanced Concepts**

1. **transformer**
   - What: Attention-based models
   - Learn: `learn transformer`

2. **reinforcement_learning**
   - What: Learning through trial and error
   - Learn: `learn reinforcement_learning`
